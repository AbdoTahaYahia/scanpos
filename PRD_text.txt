1. Product Overview
ScanPos is a mobile point-of-sale (POS) and inventory management app designed for 
supermarkets. It allows cashiers to scan product barcodes or QR codes, calculate totals, and 
process sales — while giving managers and warehouse staff full control over the product 
catalog and stock levels.
1.1 Core Value Proposition
•
Fast, frictionless scanning-based checkout experience
•
Real-time inventory tracking — stock auto-decrements on every sale
•
Role-based access for managers, warehouse staff, and cashiers
•
Clean black-and-white UI with rounded, modern design language
1.2 Design Principles
•
Color palette: Pure black & white only — no color accents
•
Shape language: Rounded corners, circular elements, soft curves throughout
•
Minimalist navigation: Bottom bar with exactly two tabs — Scanner and Inventory
•
Mobile-first: Designed for phones held in one hand at a checkout counter

2.1 Manager
•
Creates the store account via Google Sign-In
•
Gets a unique store invite code to share with employees
•
Assigns each employee a role (Cashier or Warehouse Staff) after they join
•
Has full access to all features including sales history
2.2 Warehouse Staff
•
Signs in with Google, selects 'Employee', enters the manager's invite code
•
Can browse, add, edit, and update all product data and stock quantities
•
Cannot view sales history or manage other employees
2.3 Cashier
•
Signs in with Google, selects 'Employee', enters the manager's invite code
•
Can only scan products and process sales
•
Cannot view or modify inventory or sales history

3. Authentication Flow
3.1 First-Time Sign-In
Step 1 — Google Sign-In: User taps 'Sign in with Google' and authenticates.
Step 2 — Role Selection: User is presented with two options:
–
I'm a Manager — Create a new store
–
I'm an Employee — Join an existing store
Step 3a (Manager): Store profile is created. A unique invite code is generated and shown to the 
manager.
Step 3b (Employee): User enters the invite code. The system matches them to the store. 
Manager assigns their role (Cashier or Warehouse Staff).
3.2 Returning Users
•
Google Sign-In automatically restores the user's role and store context
•
No re-entry of invite codes required

4. Screens & Features
4.1 Scanner Screen (QR Tab)
This is the default home screen for all users. It opens the device camera and is ready to scan 
immediately on launch.
Scanning Behavior
•
Camera opens automatically when the tab is active
•
Scans standard barcodes (EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A) and QR codes printed on products
•
On successful scan, the product is looked up from the store's inventory by barcode
•
If found: product name, price, and image are added to the current session cart
•
If not found: a clear error message is shown — 'Product not in inventory'
Cart & Total
•
Each scanned product appears in a running list below the scanner
•
The total amount updates in real time as items are added
•
Each item in the list shows: product name, unit price, quantity scanned
•
User can remove an item from the cart by swiping or tapping a remove button
Pay Button
•
A prominent 'Pay' button is always visible at the bottom of the screen
•
Tapping 'Pay' confirms the sale
•
The transaction is saved to sales history with: date, time, list of items, and total
•
Stock is automatically decremented for each sold product
•
The cart is cleared and the scanner returns to idle — ready for the next customer
4.2 Inventory Screen (Warehouse Tab)
Accessible by Manager and Warehouse Staff. Cashiers do not see this tab.
Product List
•
Displays all products in the store, filterable by category
•
Each product card shows: thumbnail image, name, category, price, stock quantity
•
Search bar to find products by name or barcode number
Add Product

---PAGE---
•
Floating action button opens 'Add Product' form
•
Fields: Product Name (required), Price (required), Quantity in Stock (required), Barcode 
Number (required), Category (required), Product Image (optional — from camera or 
gallery)
•
Barcode field can be filled by typing or by scanning with the camera
Edit Product
•
Tapping any product opens an edit sheet with all fields pre-filled
•
Manager and Warehouse Staff can update: name, price, quantity, image, category
•
Deleting a product removes it from inventory (with confirmation dialog)
4.3 Sales History (Manager Only)
Accessed via a dedicated section inside the Manager's profile or settings — not a bottom tab.
•
Lists all completed transactions in reverse chronological order
•
Each transaction shows: transaction ID, date & time, cashier name, number of items, 
total amount
•
Tapping a transaction expands it to show the full item breakdown
•
Manager can scroll and browse — no deletion or editing of history
4.4 Settings & Team Management (Manager Only)
•
View all employees linked to the store
•
Change any employee's role (Cashier ↔ Warehouse Staff)
•
Regenerate or copy the store invite code
•
Remove an employee from the store

7. Scope
7.1 In Scope (V1)
•
Google Sign-In authentication
•
Three-role system: Manager, Warehouse Staff, Cashier
•
Barcode and QR code scanning using device camera
•
Real-time cart with total and pay flow
•
Full inventory management (add, edit, delete products)
•
Auto stock deduction on sale
•
Sales history for managers
•
Invite code onboarding for employees
•
Black & white rounded UI
7.2 Out of Scope (Future Versions)
•
Payment gateway integration (cash-only in V1)
•
Receipt printing or PDF export
•
Barcode/QR code generation for custom labels
•
Analytics dashboard and sales reports
•
Multi-branch / multi-store support
•
Low stock alerts and notifications
•
Customer loyalty or discount system
